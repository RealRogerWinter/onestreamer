Status: Ready

How to Test the dB Meter:

  • Open the streaming application: Navigate to https://onestreamer.live
  • Start streaming: Click the "Start Streaming" button
  • Check the dB meter: Look for the audio level meter at the bottom of the video
  • Test with microphone: Speak into your microphone and watch the meter respond
  • Test with generated tone: Use the button above to generate a test tone

What to Look For:

  • Green levels (-60 to -20 dB): Safe, quiet audio
  • Yellow levels (-20 to -6 dB): Optimal audio levels
  • Orange levels (-6 to -3 dB): Caution, getting loud
  • Red levels (-3 to 0 dB): Risk of clipping/distortion
  • Peak indicator: White line showing recent peak level
  • Real-time response: Meter should react instantly to audio changes
